**Python Data Types**

**Numeric**	
int, float, complex	
holds numeric values

**String**	
str	
holds sequence of characters

**Sequence**
list, tuple, range	
holds collection of items

**Mapping**	
dict	
holds data in key-value pair form

**Boolean**	
bool	
holds either True or False

**Set**	
set, frozeenset	
hold collection of unique items
